Our Mission

Rebuilding Lives, Empowering Communities

Hurricane Mellissa devastated Jamaica on October 28, 2025, leaving thousands homeless, hungry, and hopeless. The Allwyn Facey Foundation is responding with immediate humanitarian action.

We are shipping a full 20ft container of relief supplies from Perpignan, France to Jamaica, reaching four severely affected parishes: Saint Mary, Trelawny, Westmoreland, and Saint Elizabeth.

Together, we rebuild Jamaica. Together, we rise.

The container is scheduled to ship by November 30, 2025, arriving in Jamaica in December 2025 for immediate distribution to affected parishes.

Relief supplies will be distributed to Saint Mary, Trelawny, Westmoreland, and Saint Elizabeth‚Äîthe four parishes most severely affected by Hurricane Mellissa.
